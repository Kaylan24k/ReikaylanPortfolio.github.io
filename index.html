My BookMate Navigator
(Python)

Recommendation Search Screen

TF-ID Vector

  • Click here to view dataset
  • Click here to view Project Write-up

My BookMate Navigator is a user-friendly book recommendation app designed for BookMate, a bookstore with 10,000 books. The app helps users find books they'll love by suggesting similar ones based on descriptions and genres. It uses TF-IDF and K-nearest neighbors models programmed in Python on Google Colab for efficiency. Instead of complex models like LSTM and GRU, TF-IDF and KNN strike a balance between accuracy and speed. The system uses Python, Google Colab, and libraries like Pandas for easy collaboration. In user surveys, 85% expressed extreme satisfaction, and 70% found the recommendations accurate. Some users felt the app could do better in introducing them to new authors or genres (30%). Overall, My BookMate Navigator offers a simple, effective way to discover books you'll enjoy.
